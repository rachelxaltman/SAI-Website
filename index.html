Sigma Alpha Iota at The University
of North Carolina at Charlotte

The University of North Carolina at Charlotte is located in Charlotte, North Carolina. Sigma Alpha Iota (SAI) is an international music fraternity which encourages friendship among people who share a common love for music. The Mu Psi chapter at UNCC is in Region VI and Province Tau B. Our chapter at UNCC currently has 16 collegiate members. The sisters of UNCC's chapter welcome you to explore our site!
